MangroveVision

MangroveVision is an advanced image recognition system designed to predict classification of mangrove species found in the Philippines. Using machine learning and computer vision, it analyzes images of mangroves to predict their species.

Start Detection

Why Choose MangroveVision?

Mangroves are essential for coastal protection, biodiversity, and carbon storage. However, identifying different mangrove species can be difficult due to their similar features. MangroveVision simplifies this process by offering:

✅ Automated species prediction – No expert knowledge required.
✅ Fast detection results – Instantly recognizes various mangrove species.
✅ Accessible to everyone – Useful for students, scientists, and local communities.

How It Works

1️⃣ Upload an Image – Capture or upload a mangrove photo.
2️⃣ AI Recognition – The system analyzes it using AI.
3️⃣ Get Results – Instantly predict the species with details.
